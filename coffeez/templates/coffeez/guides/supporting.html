How to Support Creators

New to crypto? This guide will walk you through every step to send a donation using TRX on Coffeez.

What You’ll Need

  • A small amount of TRX (Tron) cryptocurrency
  • A TRON-compatible wallet (mobile app, browser extension)

Good News: You do not need a Coffeez account to support a creator.

Step-by-Step: Sending Your Donation

Notice: TRX Network Fees (Gas)
Every TRX transaction requires a small network fee, called gas. This fee pays for processing your transaction on the blockchain.

What does this mean for you?

  • The fee is usually very low (often less than 1 TRX), but you must have enough TRX in your wallet to cover both your donation and the fee.
  • To be safe, buy a little more TRX than you plan to send. For example, if you want to donate 2 TRX, consider buying 3 TRX.
  • Your wallet will show the fee before you confirm the transaction.
Note: Coffeez takes no cut from your donation. The only deduction is the TRX network fee, which goes to the blockchain, not Coffeez or the creator.

  1. Get TRX (Tron):
    • Buy TRX on a trusted crypto exchange (Some wallets also do this)
    • Follow the exchange’s instructions to create an account and purchase TRX.
    • Transfer your TRX to your own wallet app or extension for sending.
  2. Go to the Creator’s Coffeez page:
    • Find the creator’s link (e.g., https://coffeez.xyz/creatorname).
    • Click Support and choose how many “coffees” you want to buy.
    • Enter your name and an optional message.
  3. See the payment details:
    • After submitting, you’ll see a page showing:
      • The exact TRX amount to send (e.g., 2.0137 TRX)
      • The creator’s wallet address (looks like T...)
      • A timer showing how long you have to complete the donation
  4. Send TRX from your wallet:
    • Open your TRON wallet app or extension.
    • Choose Send or Transfer.
    • Paste the creator’s wallet address into the Recipient field.
    • Enter the exact TRX amount shown on Coffeez. Do not round or change the amount!
    • Double-check the address and amount, then confirm/send.
    Warning: Only send TRX using the TRON network (TRC20). Sending TRX from other networks (like Ethereum or Binance Smart Chain) will result in lost funds and cannot be recovered.
  5. Wait for confirmation:
    • Return to the Coffeez page. It will automatically check for your donation every 30 seconds.
    • Once received, you’ll see a Thank You message and your transaction ID.

Tips & Safety

  • Always send the exact TRX amount shown. This lets Coffeez verify your donation.
  • Double-check the wallet address. Crypto transactions cannot be reversed.
  • If the timer expires, do not send TRX. Start a new donation instead.
  • If you need help, contact support.

Reminder: All donations are voluntary and final.
