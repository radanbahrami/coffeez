How to Move Funds

Why move your funds? For best security, Coffeez recommends occasionally moving your funds to a new address that you control. This helps protect your assets in case your old private key is ever exposed or compromised.

What You’ll Need

  • Your current TRON wallet and private key
  • A new TRON-compatible wallet (mobile app, browser extension, or hardware wallet)

Step-by-Step: Moving Your Funds

  1. Create a new wallet:
    • Download a trusted TRON wallet app or extension.
    • Follow the instructions to create a new wallet and securely save your new private key or seed phrase.
  2. Get your new wallet address:
    • Copy the new wallet’s TRON address (starts with T...).
  3. Send your funds:
    • Open your old wallet and choose Send.
    • Paste your new wallet address as the recipient.
    • Enter the amount of TRX you want to move (leave a small amount for network fees).
    • Double-check the address and amount, then confirm/send.
      • Warning: Only send TRX using the TRON network (TRC20). Sending TRX from other networks (like Ethereum or Binance Smart Chain) will result in lost funds and cannot be recovered.
  4. Verify your funds:
    • Open your new wallet and confirm the TRX has arrived.

Tips & Safety

  • Always double-check the new wallet address. Crypto transactions cannot be reversed.
  • Keep your new private key or seed phrase safe and never share it.

Reminder: Moving funds to a new wallet is optional but recommended for long-term security.
