About Coffeez

Hi, I’m Radan, the creator of Coffeez, a platform born from my love of coffee and a passion for creating useful software.

Coffeez is a simple, transparent micro-donations platform that lets creators receive instant, uncut, and borderless payments. Built with a non-custodial wallet system, it ensures creators always stay in full control of their funds.

Our focus is on speed, ease of use, and respect for user privacy, making it effortless for both creators and supporters to connect and transact.

Coffeez is opensource and the source is available on GitHub.

If you have any suggestions, feedback, or ideas for collaboration, please don’t hesitate to reach out.

I hope you enjoy!

Radan Bahrami
