Frequently Asked Questions

How can I trust Coffeez?
Coffeez is open-source and uses a non-custodial system. That means we never have access to your funds or private keys. You can review the code on GitHub and see exactly how Coffeez works. Your wallet stays in your control, always.
What does non-custodial mean?
Non-custodial means Coffeez never has access to your wallet's private key. That key is what allows access to your crypto funds. All payments go directly from the sender to the recipient's wallet, without being held by us.
What crypto is used?
Coffeez uses TRX (Tron) because of its low transaction fees, fast confirmations, and reliable blockchain infrastructure.
Why not Bitcoin or Ethereum?
Bitcoin and Ethereum are great, but they have higher transaction fees and slower confirmation times. That makes them less suitable for small, frequent payments like digital donations or tips.
Are there any fees?
Coffeez charges zero platform fees. The only cost is the small network (gas) fee required by the Tron blockchain to process your transaction.
Do I need a wallet to use Coffeez?
No. We will create a wallet for you using our secure API. However, we recommend that you occasionally transfer your funds to a personal wallet for better security and long-term storage.
Can anyone become a Coffeez member?
Yes, anyone can join Coffeez, as long as they follow our Terms of Service. Creators must not use the platform for illegal or explicit activity, including but not limited to money laundering, scams, adult content, or anything that violates local laws. Read more

Coffeez is developed and maintained by Radan Bahrami. You can learn more about us on the about page.
Is it free?
Yes. Coffeez is 100% free to use, with no hidden charges.
